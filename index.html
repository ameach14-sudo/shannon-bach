What to pack 🧳

☀️ SPF 50+ sunscreen — reapply constantly
💧 Water bottle — stay hydrated all day
👙 Swimsuit — the pool will be your best friend
🕶️ Sunglasses & a hat for outdoor activities
🌬️ Light, breathable fabrics (linen, cotton)
🧥 Light layer for A/C — restaurants are cold inside
⛈️ Small umbrella for afternoon pop-up storms
🤠 Boots optional but highly encouraged for honky tonk

Pro tip: Plan outdoor activities for morning or evening. Midday (12–4pm) is brutal — that's what the pool and A/C are for. Barton Springs stays a refreshing 68°F year-round and is the perfect midday escape.
